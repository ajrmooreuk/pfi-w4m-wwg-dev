One core. Many deployments. Lego-brick by design.

LSC is built from PFC core capabilities, licensed by PFI-W4M, and extensible by partner PFIs who can add or resell modules. Each client gets their own instance, co-branded once deployed.

Layer 1 · Core

PFC Core Capabilities

VE, Farsight, Platform Portfolio — strategy-as-code spine reused across every instance.

Layer 2 · PFI

PFI-W4M Licensee

wings4mind owns the LSC product line, packages it, and licenses it to partner PFIs and direct clients.

Layer 3 · Partner

Partner PFIs

Add vertical modules, resell, or co-deliver. The lego bricks stay snap-compatible with the core.

Layer 4 · Client

Client Instance

Each client gets a dedicated instance, co-branded on deployment with their colours, logo and language.
